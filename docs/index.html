Privacy Policy for Cubicatch

Effective Date: December 26, 2024

Thank you for choosing to play Cubicatch! Your privacy and trust are important to us, and we are committed to maintaining a safe and secure experience for all players. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of your information.

No Data Collection by Cubicatch

Cubicatch itself does not collect, store, or process any personal or non-personal data from its users. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Physical addresses
  • IP addresses
  • Device information
  • Usage data
  • Cookies or tracking technologies

Unity Engine

Cubicatch is built using the Unity game engine. While we do not utilize any Unity services that actively collect data (such as analytics or ads), we cannot guarantee that Unity’s base engine does not collect any information. For details on Unity’s data collection practices, please review their privacy policy here.

Offline Gameplay

Cubicatch is designed to be played offline and does not require an internet connection for its core functionalities. As such, there are no mechanisms in place to transmit any data from your device by the game itself.

Third-Party Services

Cubicatch does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks that might collect data on our behalf. The game operates independently and ensures your privacy to the extent of our control.

Children's Privacy

Cubicatch is suitable for players of all ages. As we do not collect any data directly, there is no risk of compromising the privacy of children or any other players. However, we encourage parents and guardians to review Unity's privacy practices for further understanding.

Updates to the Privacy Policy

While our commitment to not collecting data remains steadfast, we reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the game or applicable laws. Any changes will be communicated through an updated version of this document. Please review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to reach out to us at adam.markus.jonsson@gmail.com.

Thank you for playing Cubicatch! Enjoy the game with complete peace of mind knowing that your privacy is fully protected to the extent of our capabilities.
